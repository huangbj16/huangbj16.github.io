Hi, I'm Bingjian(Alex) Huang

Currently I'm a junior student studying Computer Science in Tsinghua University, Beijing, China

I have great interest in understanding human beings, especially human's interactions with "things". I would like to know how people interact with their daily-life devices, what are the existing limits, and what we can do improve them. That's why I step into the field of Human-Computer Interaction(HCI), which is also called Human-Centered Design(HCD).

I have been doing research in HCI since April, 2018 when I joined the Key Laboratary of Pervasive Computing (Advisor: Prof. Yuanchun Shi, Prof. Chun Yu) in Tsinghua University. Since then, I have participated in three different research projects related to Virtual Reality(VR), Telepresence, Wearable devices, gesture design and so on.

Currently, I am working as a summer intern in Inteco Lab (Advisor: Prof. Mark W. Newman) of School of Information, University of Michigan, Ann Arbor. I am collaborating with PhD candidate Xinghui Yan towards the research project about low-burden in-situ self-reporting.
